Welcome to Sarvangin Vikas Sanstha (SVS)

Sarvangin Vikas Sanstha (SVS) is a registered non-profit organization. The main objective of SVS is the "women empowerment". We believe that 'Education' can be the best channel to empower the women in our society. We are presently working in the Pune city & we have started the program of "Adult Literacy" in the slum area at Pashan, Pune named "Sanjay Gandhi Vasahat". Our objective is to make the slum completely literate by year December 2015. We have also played major role in developing open source educational softwares in Marathi language. These softwares have been distributed in around 1000 schools of Dhule & Wardha district.
