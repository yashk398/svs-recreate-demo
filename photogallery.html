SVS India has started the adult literacy program in Sanjay Gandhi Vasahat, Pashan, Pune.

Photo Gallery

Sarvangin Vikas Sanstha (SVS) is taking efforts to achieve 100% literacy in 'Sanjay Gandhi Vasahat', Pashan, Pune. We have prepared the team of 13 teachers to teach 100 illiterate women. But before starting the literacy classes, we trained the teachers. We conducted the 2 days workshop to teachers & now they have been trained to teach illiterate women.
